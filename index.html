    Why not participate in our photo of the season competition? Submit any photos relating to yachting and boating (You do not have to have chartered a boat from KaterBlue to participate!). The photo should be funny, beautiful, memorable or simply meaningful. This should be accompanied by a paragraph detailing where the photo was taken, and little background information explaining why it is close to your heart. This competition is open to all registered users of KaterBlue and there is no registration cost if you are not yet a member. You must register your profile or a boat (or boath) and submit at leaste one photo and your story. The top ten photos will be listed here and one winner of the "Photo of the Season" will be announced here. All will be informed by email. The submitter of the best photo of the season will win a week's boat charter in a well sought-after destination free of charge. The deadline for summer 2019 submissions ends October 14th 2019.

    Boat/Yacht Listing for Charter or Sale

    Do you own or manage a watercraft? List them here for free; either for sale or charter. It will be visible to wide range off community looking for your listing right away.

    Charter or Sale your watercraft at ease. You set your own rates, and can send a custom offer. You will be generating a spare income to cover your operational costs.

    You are secured with the future cancellations, damages when chartered, and can even monetise by allowing a trial charter for people looking to buy your watercraft.

    Renter's/Buyer's Perspective

    Boat/Yacht Search for Charter or Purchase

    A wide-range of boats and yachts are available for charter or purchase here at KaterBlue. You can make your desired custom search and find the best match either to take out on a charter or buy it at a best re-conditioned price.

    After you have made the choice, you are connected with the owner direclty. You can book rightaway or inquire as you need more info. You have the flexibility of modifying, changing, cancelling the reservation anytime you want.

    You are just a click away to chartering or buying a boat or yacht through a trustworthy online platform in Europe and beyond. You can stay calm and just sail on. We take care of your complete security from the time you board a boat to the moment you get off of it.
